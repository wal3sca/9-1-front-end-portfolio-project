HELLO, Welcome to Meals!

This app is a search generator for different meals based on category. For example, you can look up specific dishes that include, pasta, beef, chicken or even vegan meals!

On this site, you'll be able to:

  • Type into a search form where you can look for meals by category.
  • After you've clicked the search button, you'll retrieve images and a description of the meal.
