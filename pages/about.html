HELLO! Welcome to Meals

This app is a search generator for different meals based on category (i.e. Pasta, Seafood, Chicken, Vegan etc.) On this site, you'll be able to: Type into a search form where you can look for meals when I click the search button. - As a user I can click on the search button to then retrieve a list of different meals by category (i.e Seafood, Chicken, Beef etc). - As a user, I am able to see images of different meals that I've selected show up on the page.
